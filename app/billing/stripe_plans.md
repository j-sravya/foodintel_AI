# Stripe Billing Plan

Stripe is not required for the academic MVP, but it strengthens the production SaaS model.

## Suggested Plans

| Plan | Buyer | Features |
|---|---|---|
| Starter | Small vendor teams | 3 users, 100 briefs/month, pitch downloads, basic CRM notes |
| Growth | Regional sales teams | 15 users, 1,000 briefs/month, team dashboard, PostHog analytics, bulk prospect upload |
| Enterprise | Large distributors / national suppliers | Unlimited seats, custom integrations, live APIs, SSO, advanced audit logs |

## Billable Limits

- Number of sales users
- Briefs generated per month
- Pitch document downloads
- CRM integrations
- Live data connectors
- Advanced competitor intelligence
